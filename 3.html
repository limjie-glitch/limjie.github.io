Moving Forward...

4 months into coding, i received the invitation to my first coding assessment that a company gives to assess your ability to learn and pick up skills in the tech industry. The position i applied for was a Software graduate programme, and they offer a 6 weeks training in Java to help you attain skills that are required of you in the role.

Upon completing the assessment, i received a call from the assessor, saying that i qualified for the position, and asked me to confirm with them my availability to work. Sadly, the latest programme was too soon for me to attend as i need to serve a notice to my current job.

Wth that experience on coding assessment, i really plucked up my courage to go for more assessments, from Data Science Bootcamps, to Java Developer Graduate programmes, and i am currently waiting for my application to get back to me.

Looking back, it has been a hell of a journey, and it has certainly been wild. But, you cannot consider youself as having lived your life if you live it the safe way right?
